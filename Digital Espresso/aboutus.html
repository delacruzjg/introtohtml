About Digital Espresso

Digital Espresso was founded in 2010 in the heart of downtown, driven by a passion for artisan coffee and a commitment to community building. We source our beans from local farmers and internationally recognized plantations committed to sustainable farming practices.

Our team comprises expert baristas who not only excel in coffee preparation but also in customer service, ensuring that each visit is a memorable experience. We offer a wide variety of coffee blends and brewing methods, catering to connoisseurs and casual coffee drinkers alike.

Beyond coffee, we host monthly events that range from barista training sessions to live music nights, creating a vibrant community space for everyone to enjoy. Our mission is to bring people together over great coffee and great conversation.
